# ARCHITECTURE_PRINCIPLES.md

# SirajOS Architectural Principles

## Principle 1

Knowledge is the primary asset.

Everything else is derived.

---

## Principle 2

Every concept has one canonical implementation.

---

## Principle 3

Domain is independent.

It never depends on infrastructure.

---

## Principle 4

Workflow coordinates.

It never owns business logic.

---

## Principle 5

Knowledge precedes narrative.

Narrative precedes production.

---

## Principle 6

Every module has one responsibility.

---

## Principle 7

Builders construct.

Services execute.

Repositories persist.

Generators produce.

---

## Principle 8

The Knowledge Graph is the semantic heart of SirajOS.

---

## Principle 9

Architecture evolves deliberately.

Never accidentally.

---

## Principle 10

Every architectural decision must be documented before implementation.

---

## Principle 11

Refactoring must preserve observable behavior.

---

## Principle 12

The system is designed for extensibility.

Future engines (Books, Podcasts, Courses, Interactive Timelines) must integrate without changing the Domain.

---

## Principle 13

External providers are replaceable.

Business knowledge is permanent.

---

## Principle 14

Documentation is part of the source code.

---

## Principle 15

A clean architecture today saves hundreds of hours tomorrow.
