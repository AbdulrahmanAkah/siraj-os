Version: 1.0.0-draft

Status: ACTIVE

# ARTICLE 1

Mission

Siraj exists to become the world's most accurate AI-assisted Islamic historical production platform.

Accuracy is always more important than entertainment.

Every production must increase knowledge while respecting Islamic ethics.

---

# ARTICLE 2

Vision

To build an operating system capable of producing historically accurate Islamic documentaries from the creation of Adam عليه السلام until the Day of Judgment.

The platform must eventually become capable of producing books, documentaries, educational series, interactive knowledge systems and future media formats.

# ARTICLE 4

Historical Principles

Historical claims must be supported by reliable sources.

Differences between historians shall be documented.

Weak narrations shall never be presented as established facts.

Whenever scholarly disagreement exists, it must be made explicit.

No historical event may be exaggerated for dramatic purposes.

Chronology must remain internally consistent.

---

# ARTICLE 5

Islamic Principles

Prophets are never visually depicted.

No facial features shall appear for Prophets under any circumstance.

Sacred historical figures may appear only according to approved visual rules.

No music.

No content contradicting authentic Islamic teachings.

No sensationalism.

No mockery.

No disrespect.

---

# ARTICLE 6

Visual Production

Historical environments must match the real historical period.

Architecture, geography, vegetation, climate, clothing, weapons, animals and transportation must be historically researched.

Visual beauty must never replace historical accuracy.

---

# ARTICLE 7

Knowledge Before Production

Research comes before writing.

Writing comes before storyboard.

Storyboard comes before prompts.

Prompts come before production.

Production comes before review.

Review comes before publishing.

---

# ARTICLE 8

Documentation

Every important decision must be documented.

Every source must remain traceable.

Every generated asset must have provenance.

No undocumented change is considered official.

---

# ARTICLE 9

AI Philosophy

Artificial Intelligence is an assistant.

It never replaces scholarly verification.

Every AI output is considered a draft until reviewed.

Multiple AI systems may cooperate.

No single AI model is considered authoritative.

---

# ARTICLE 10

Continuous Improvement

Siraj is designed as a living system.

Every workflow may evolve.

Every document may improve.

The Constitution is the highest authority inside the project.

Any future component conflicting with this Constitution must be redesigned.
