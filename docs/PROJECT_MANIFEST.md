## Primary Mission

Build the world's most accurate AI-assisted Islamic historical production system.

The first production built on this platform is:

**Siraj**
(A documentary-style Islamic historical series from the creation of Adam عليه السلام until the Day of Judgment.)

---

## Core Principles

- Historical accuracy before entertainment.
- No fabrication of historical facts.
- Authentic Islamic sources only.
- No depiction of Prophets.
- No visible facial features for sacred historical figures when required.
- No music.
- Respect Islamic ethics in every production stage.
- Every generated asset must be traceable to its sources.
- Every decision must be documented.

---

## Long-Term Vision

SirajOS will become an AI Operating System capable of producing:

- Historical documentaries
- Educational series
- Scientific productions
- Knowledge projects

through specialized AI agents working together under one architecture.
