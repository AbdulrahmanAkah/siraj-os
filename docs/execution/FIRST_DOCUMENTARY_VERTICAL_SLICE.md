## Subject

The Creation of Adam: What the Islamic Sources Establish

## Source hierarchy

1. Quran.
2. Authenticated hadith.
3. Reviewed classical tafsir.
4. Reviewed classical historical works.
5. Modern research only as explanatory commentary.

Every claim must record:

- Source identity.
- Exact evidence.
- Source location.
- Claim status.
- Confidence.
- Review decision.
- Contradictions or alternative reports.

## Visual religious-safety policy

The production must not depict:

- Allah.
- Prophets.
- Angels as embodied characters.
- Sacred unseen events as asserted physical reconstructions.

Permitted visual strategies include:

- Environments.
- Landscapes.
- Objects.
- Maps.
- Timelines.
- Architecture.
- Calligraphic or textual presentation.
- Abstract light, particles, silhouettes without identity, and symbolic motion,
  subject to human review.
- Source quotations and evidence cards.
