# ADR-001 — Knowledge First

Status: Accepted

## Decision

Siraj Platform adopts a Knowledge-First architecture.

Knowledge is the foundation of the system.
All reasoning, production and publishing depend on it.

## Rationale

Knowledge is the only permanent asset.

AI models, databases and tools may change.

The knowledge model must remain stable.

## Consequences

- Build the Knowledge Model before AI agents.
- Separate knowledge from presentation.
- Separate claims from sources.
- Every output must be traceable to evidence.
